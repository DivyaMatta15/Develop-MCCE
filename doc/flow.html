Python MCCE

A Python version of Multi-Conformation Continuum Electrostatics simulation program to model protein electrostatic interactions.

Program Mechanism

A protein or protein complex structure is broken down into managable pieces, usually amino acid residues and cofactors. Each piece may have charge, protonation, and position conformers. These peices interact with each other, and the piece conformers are sampled by Monte Carlo sampling to find their probabilty of occupancy at various conditions, such as pH, redox potention, chemical potential, and introduced charges.
