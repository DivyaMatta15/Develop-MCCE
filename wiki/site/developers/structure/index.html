Main program: pymcce

Four steps: premcce.py: convert to mcce pdb mkconf.py: make conformers mkenergy.py: caculate energy table monte.py: Monte Carlo sampling of states

Supporting modules: pymccelib.py: mcce modules geometry.py: geometry operations

Analysis tools: fitpka.py: Titration curve fitting mfe.py: use mean field to analyze free energy
